Frequently Asked Questions

What is German Noun-Genders?

German Noun-Gender is a Chrome extension that helps you learn German nouns and their genders passively by displaying a new word with visual cues every time you open a new tab on your desktop.

How does the gender colour-coding work?

Each noun is displayed with a background colour that indicates its gender. Blue is for males, green is for females, and grey is for gender-neutral words. This helps you easily remember the gender of each word.

Is the extension free to use?

We offer a free plan that gives you lifetime access to 50 random words from the official A1 and A2 lists. For more extensive access, you can purchase the A1 or A2 Level plans.

Can I switch between different levels?

Yes! If you purchase the A2 Level plan, you can switch between the A1 and A2 levels as per your choice.

How is the payment structured?

All plans are billed only once, meaning you make a one-time payment for lifetime access.

Will there be more levels available in the future?

Yes, we will be launching the B1 level soon. You can sign up for the waitlist to get exclusive discounts on launch day.

How can I get the extension?

You can install the extension directly from the Chrome Web Store and start learning German nouns immediately as you browse the web.
